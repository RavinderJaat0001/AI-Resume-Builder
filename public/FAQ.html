Frequently Asked Questions

Here you'll find everything you need to know about how to create professional resume easily , CVs and cover letters.

How do I create a resume using this platform?

To create a resume, simply select a template from the available options. Then, fill in the necessary details like your name, contact information, education, skills, experience, and more using the form provided. Once completed, you can preview your resume, make adjustments, and download it in your preferred format.

Can I customize the resume templates?

Yes! You can customize the templates by changing fonts, colors, and layouts. Additionally, you can add or remove sections based on your needs. This flexibility allows you to create a resume that best showcases your qualifications.

Is there an option to add additional sections to my resume?

Absolutely! You can add custom sections like certifications, volunteer experience, projects, or hobbies. Just navigate to the “Add Section” button and choose the type of content you want to include.

Can I edit my resume after downloading it?

Once you download the resume, it will be in a PDF format, which is not editable directly. However, you can return to your account, make changes using the editor, and download the updated version at any time.

What file formats are available for download?

Currently, resumes can be downloaded in PDF format, ensuring compatibility with most job application systems. In the future, we may introduce additional formats like Word (.docx) or plain text.

How does the AI enhancement feature work?
>

The AI enhancement feature analyzes your content, corrects grammatical errors, and suggests improvements for clarity and conciseness. This can help make your resume more impactful and professional. Simply click on “Enhance with AI” for instant improvements.

Is the platform free to use?

Our platform offers a free plan that includes essential resume-building features. For additional customization options and premium templates, you can subscribe to our affordable premium plan.

How can I ensure my resume stands out?
>

To make your resume stand out, focus on highlighting your achievements with quantifiable results. Use strong action verbs, tailor your resume to the job you’re applying for, and maintain a clean, professional design. The AI suggestions can further optimize your resume.

Can I use this resume builder for different industries?

Yes! We offer templates suitable for various industries like IT, marketing, finance, healthcare, and more. Customize sections and content to align your resume with industry-specific expectations.

Is my data safe on this platform?

We take your privacy seriously. Your data is encrypted and securely stored. You can delete your information at any time from your account settings. Additionally, we do not share your personal data with third parties.
